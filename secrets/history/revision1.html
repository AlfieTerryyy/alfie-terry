Treaty of Versailles & League of Nations

What was the Treaty of Versailles?
A peace treaty that ended WWI, blaming Germany and imposing reparations. This also made France annoyed because they wanted more damage done to Germany.
Main goal of the League of Nations?
To keep world peace and prevent wars.
Why did the US not join?
The Senate rejected it, fearing loss of control.
Who were the "Big Three"?
Leaders of the US - Woodrow Wilson, UK - David George, and France - Georges Clemenceau.
